Brief Introduction About Myself


Hello, I’m Wai Zhen and I’m an aspiring data scientist/data analyst from Selangor, Malaysia. I’m a recent Master’s degree graduate in the field of Data Science from University of Malaya in Kuala Lumpur.

I used to study Civil Engineering for my degree but I have always wanted to be part of the technology revolution that uses innovation to create amazing ideas. This motivated me to leave the engineering world and transition into data science, shaping who I am today.

My background in engineering enables me to learn statistics and applied programming skills which really helps me throughout my transition into data science. Being a person who is not from computer science background, I realized that I need to put more effort in improving my skills and working on projects is the best way to achieve this.

To bring myself closer to my goal of becoming a successful data scientist, I have taken multiple online learning courses and completed a few data science projects in my free time to polish my data skills. I enjoy learning new things about data and strive to establish myself in the career of data science.

My Passion in Data

The part that I like the most while doing data science is the fact that I’m able to produce insights from raw data, and being able to produce machine learning models that successfully predicts certain outcomes that add values grants me the feeling of satisfaction which I love a lot.

My Personal Accomplishments

My biggest personal accomplishment in my opinion is that I managed to complete 2 personal projects from raw data to deploying the models by myself without seeking help from anyone. One of my master’s degree visualisation project app has also been praised by the lecturer for being simple and comprehensive.

What I am Looking For

Upon graduation, I am currently looking for job opportunities in data science and machine learning. My main priority would be seeking for data scientist roles while data analyst positions would be my second choice. I am available to start work from 27 September 2021 onwards.
